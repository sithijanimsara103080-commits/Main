01 About Us

The R.A.S.

The Radapasa Astronomical Society, established in 2013, stands as a beacon of cosmic curiosity. For nearly 12 years, we have been dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the universe through workshops, observation camps, and academic excellence.

Our mission extends beyond the classroom. We organize annual star parties, participate in national Olympiads, and foster a community where every student can gaze upwards and dream. From the Moon to the deep cosmos, our journey is eternal.

- From The Astronomical Team
